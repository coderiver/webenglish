Preparation for the Test

Now we offer you to pass a short test to check your interpreting skills.

The system will select at random 10 dialogues for you to simultaneously translate. Each dialogue consists of several parts in the languages selected by you. After playing each part of the dialogue, the system will turn on automatically the recording function, without stopping, and prompt you to translate the part. You will be given an equal amount of time to translate as the taken by the original plus 5 seconds. Your test will be reviewed within one day. After reviewing your test we will contact you for further instructions.

If you are approved, you will get access to your personal account and start earning money.
